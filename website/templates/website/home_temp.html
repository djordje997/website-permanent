O nama About us

Osnovani smo 1999. godine u Novom Sadu, a od 2004. godine se bavimo isključivo računovodstvenom, poresko-savetodavnom i konsultantskom delatnošću. Do danas smo ostvarili uspešnu saradnju sa velikim brojem klijenata, iz Novog Sada, ali isto tako I sa nemalim brojem onih koji su van Novog Sada i iz inostranstva. Nastojimo da koristeći internet i savremena IT rešenja, pružimo profesionalne i ažurne usluge svima bez obzira na geografske odrednice.

 We are founded in 1999 in Novi Sad, and since 2004. we are dealing exclusively with accounting, tax - advisory and consulting work. Until today we have accomplished succesful cooperation with vast number of clients, both from Novi Sad and out of Novi Sad, but also from abroad. We attempt to use the Internet and modern IT solutions, in a way to provide professional and up-to-date services to everyone, regardless of the geographical location.

Našu profesionalnu uslugu nastojimo kontinuirano unapređivati otvorenom komunikacijom i proaktivnim pristupom. Stalno radimo na tome da svim klijentima obezbedimo adekvatno poresko savetovanje u okvirima pozitivnih propisa kao i asistenciju u ostvarivanju profitabilnog poslovanja. Cilj nam je da u odnosu sa klijentom osiguramo pouzdanost, sigurnost i iskrenost kako bismo kreirali istinski partnerski odnos, time smo se vodili od samog starta a tako će biti I u budućnosti, jer USPEH NAŠIH KLIJENATA JE I NAŠ USPEH. We strive to continually improve our professional service with open communication and proactive approach. We are constantly working to ensure that all clients have adequate tax consulting within the framework of positive regulations, as well as assistance in the realization of profitable business. Our main goal is to ensure reliability, security and honesty in relation to the client in order to create a genuine partnership relationship, which was our guide from the start, and so will be in the future, because the SUCCESS OF OUR CLIENTS IS OUR SUCCESS .
